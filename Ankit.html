Birthplace

Dhading is where I was born in Nepal. It has big mountains, like Ganesh Himal. The road to Kathmandu, called Prithivi Highway, goes through Dhading. People there mostly farm, and there are rivers like Budi Gandaki. Dhading has different weather, from hot to cold. Many families there are a mix of Brahmin, Chhetri, Tamang, Gurung, and Newari. It's a special place with its own culture and traditions. I grew up surrounded by nature in Dhading..
